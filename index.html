Enchanted Bath Bombs

Introduction

Enchanted Bath Bombs offer bath bombs with various shapes, sizes, and scents that can help cleanse and soothe your skin in a fun and swift way. Most of our bath bombs are inspired by the American singer-songwriter Taylor Alison Swift. Our bath bombs are made with essential oils and natural colorants that are skin-friendly and eco-friendly. We make our bath bombs based on her songs and albums that we think you Swifties would love. The scent and shape of the bath bomb is based on how we interpret and percieve a song or an album. Drop one of these Enchanted bombs in a warm relaxing bath for an explosion of color, essential oil, and fizziness. At Enchanted Bath Bombs, we want you to have the best, relaxing, and stress relieving bath you could ask for!
